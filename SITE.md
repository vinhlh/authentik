# Authentic Food Discovery - Da Nang

## 1. Site Vision

A web platform that helps people discover authentic local restaurants in Da Nang, Vietnam, filtering out fake reviews and tourist traps through curated collections from trusted local creators.

**Target Audience:**
- Expats and digital nomads in Da Nang
- Travelers planning Da Nang trips
- Locals looking for new authentic spots

**Core Value Proposition:**
"Discover where Da Nang locals actually eat" - Curated collections that surface authentic dining experiences.

## 3. Design Direction

**Visual Style:** Pinterest masonry grid + Airbnb clean minimalism

**Key Elements:**
- Masonry grid layout for dynamic browsing
- Rounded corners (12-16px) everywhere
- Generous white space and padding
- Subtle shadows
- Warm Vietnamese color palette (orange #D97706 accents)
- Photo-first approach
